# Colorectal Cancer — Treatment Overview

> Curated summary from CIViC / publicly-available guideline sources. Not official NCCN. For educational/decision-support context only.

## Targeted Therapy
- **KRAS/NRAS/BRAF wild-type, left-sided**: Anti-EGFR (cetuximab or panitumumab) + FOLFOX/FOLFIRI (Evidence: A)
- **BRAF V600E mutant**: Encorafenib + cetuximab after first-line failure (Evidence: A)
- **KRAS G12C mutant**: Sotorasib or adagrasib + cetuximab (later line) (Evidence: B)
- **HER2 amplified (RAS/BRAF wild-type)**: Trastuzumab + tucatinib or trastuzumab + pertuzumab (Evidence: B)
- **NTRK fusion**: Larotrectinib or entrectinib (Evidence: A)

## Immunotherapy
- **MSI-H / dMMR, metastatic first-line**: Pembrolizumab monotherapy (Evidence: A)
- **MSI-H / dMMR, later line**: Nivolumab ± ipilimumab (Evidence: A)
- **TMB-high (≥10 mut/Mb), MSS**: Pembrolizumab (tumor-agnostic, Evidence: B)

## Chemotherapy
- **First-line (metastatic)**: FOLFOX, FOLFIRI, or FOLFOXIRI, typically with bevacizumab
- **Second-line**: Crossover regimen ± aflibercept or ramucirumab
- **Later line**: Trifluridine/tipiracil or regorafenib
