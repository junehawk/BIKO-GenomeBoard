# Breast Cancer — Treatment Overview

> Curated summary from CIViC / publicly-available guideline sources. Not official NCCN. For educational/decision-support context only.

## Targeted Therapy
- **HR+/HER2− metastatic**: CDK4/6 inhibitor (palbociclib, ribociclib, abemaciclib) + endocrine therapy (Evidence: A)
- **PIK3CA mutant, HR+/HER2−**: Alpelisib + fulvestrant after endocrine progression (Evidence: A)
- **ESR1 mutant, HR+/HER2−**: Elacestrant (Evidence: A)
- **HER2+ early stage**: Trastuzumab + pertuzumab + chemotherapy; adjuvant T-DM1 if residual disease (Evidence: A)
- **HER2+ metastatic**: Trastuzumab deruxtecan (T-DXd), tucatinib + trastuzumab + capecitabine (Evidence: A)
- **HER2-low (IHC 1+ or 2+/ISH−)**: Trastuzumab deruxtecan (Evidence: A)
- **BRCA1/2 germline**: Olaparib or talazoparib (Evidence: A)
- **Trop-2 TNBC**: Sacituzumab govitecan (Evidence: A)
- **NTRK fusion**: Larotrectinib or entrectinib (Evidence: A)

## Immunotherapy
- **PD-L1+ (CPS ≥10) metastatic TNBC**: Pembrolizumab + chemotherapy first-line (Evidence: A)
- **Early high-risk TNBC**: Neoadjuvant pembrolizumab + chemotherapy (KEYNOTE-522) (Evidence: A)

## Chemotherapy
- **TNBC**: Anthracycline/taxane-based (AC-T), platinum in BRCA+ or high-risk
- **HR+/HER2−**: Taxane or capecitabine after endocrine progression
- **HER2+**: Paired with anti-HER2 agents
