# Epithelial Ovarian Cancer (High-Grade Serous) — Treatment Overview

> Curated summary from CIViC / publicly-available guideline sources. Not official NCCN. For educational/decision-support context only.

## Targeted Therapy
- **BRCA1/2 mutant (germline or somatic), first-line maintenance**: Olaparib ± bevacizumab (Evidence: A)
- **HRD-positive (BRCA-like), first-line maintenance**: Olaparib + bevacizumab (PAOLA-1) or niraparib (Evidence: A)
- **HRD-negative / unknown, first-line maintenance**: Niraparib (PRIMA) or bevacizumab (Evidence: A)
- **Platinum-sensitive recurrence maintenance**: Olaparib, niraparib, or rucaparib (Evidence: A)
- **Folate receptor alpha (FRα) high, platinum-resistant**: Mirvetuximab soravtansine (Evidence: A)
- **NTRK fusion**: Larotrectinib or entrectinib (Evidence: A)

## Immunotherapy
- **MSI-H / dMMR (rare)**: Pembrolizumab (Evidence: B)
- **TMB-high (≥10 mut/Mb)**: Pembrolizumab (tumor-agnostic) (Evidence: B)
- Single-agent ICI generally ineffective in HGSOC outside MSI-H/TMB-high

## Chemotherapy
- **First-line**: Carboplatin + paclitaxel (± bevacizumab); consider neoadjuvant for advanced stage
- **Platinum-sensitive recurrence**: Platinum doublet (carboplatin + gemcitabine / PLD / paclitaxel) ± bevacizumab
- **Platinum-resistant**: Single-agent (PLD, topotecan, weekly paclitaxel) ± bevacizumab
