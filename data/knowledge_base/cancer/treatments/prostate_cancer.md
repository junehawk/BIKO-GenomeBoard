# Prostate Cancer — Treatment Overview

> Curated summary from CIViC / publicly-available guideline sources. Not official NCCN. For educational/decision-support context only.

## Targeted Therapy
- **BRCA1/2 mutant (germline or somatic), mCRPC**: Olaparib (Evidence: A)
- **HRR-deficient mCRPC**: Olaparib ± abiraterone, niraparib + abiraterone, talazoparib + enzalutamide (Evidence: A)
- **AR pathway (mCSPC/mCRPC)**: Abiraterone, enzalutamide, apalutamide, darolutamide (Evidence: A)
- **PSMA-positive mCRPC (post-ARPI and taxane)**: Lu-177 PSMA-617 (Evidence: A)

## Immunotherapy
- **MSI-H / dMMR mCRPC**: Pembrolizumab (Evidence: B)
- **TMB-high (≥10 mut/Mb)**: Pembrolizumab (tumor-agnostic) (Evidence: B)
- **Sipuleucel-T**: Asymptomatic/minimally symptomatic mCRPC (Evidence: A)

## Chemotherapy
- **mCSPC high-volume**: Docetaxel + ADT ± ARPI
- **mCRPC post-ARPI**: Docetaxel, then cabazitaxel
- Taxanes remain backbone after progression on hormonal agents

## Key Biomarkers
| Gene | Variant | Drug | Evidence |
|------|---------|------|----------|
| BRCA1/2 | germline / somatic LoF | Olaparib, talazoparib | A |
| ATM, PALB2, CDK12 | LoF (HRR panel) | Olaparib (select) | B |
| MMR/MSI | dMMR / MSI-H | Pembrolizumab | B |
| AR | wild-type (signaling intact) | Enzalutamide, abiraterone | A |
| PSMA | expression (PET positive) | Lu-177 PSMA-617 | A |
