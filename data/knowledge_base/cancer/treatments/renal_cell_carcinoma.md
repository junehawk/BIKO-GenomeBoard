# Renal Cell Carcinoma (Clear Cell) — Treatment Overview

> Curated summary from CIViC / publicly-available guideline sources. Not official NCCN. For educational/decision-support context only.

## Targeted Therapy
- **VEGFR TKIs**: Sunitinib, pazopanib, cabozantinib, axitinib, lenvatinib, tivozanib (Evidence: A)
- **mTOR inhibitors**: Everolimus, temsirolimus (later line) (Evidence: A)
- **HIF-2α inhibitor**: Belzutifan (VHL disease-associated RCC; sporadic post-ICI/TKI) (Evidence: A)
- **Papillary RCC with MET alterations**: Cabozantinib or savolitinib (trial) (Evidence: B)

## Immunotherapy
- **Intermediate/poor-risk first-line**: Nivolumab + ipilimumab (Evidence: A)
- **Any risk first-line IO+TKI**: Pembrolizumab + axitinib, nivolumab + cabozantinib, or pembrolizumab + lenvatinib (Evidence: A)
- **Adjuvant (high-risk resected)**: Pembrolizumab (KEYNOTE-564) (Evidence: A)

## Chemotherapy
- Generally ineffective in clear-cell RCC
- Considered for sarcomatoid/rhabdoid variants or collecting-duct histology (gemcitabine + platinum)

## Key Biomarkers
| Gene | Variant | Drug | Evidence |
|------|---------|------|----------|
| VHL | LoF (germline or somatic) | Belzutifan | A |
| MET | amplification / mutation (papillary) | Cabozantinib, savolitinib | B |
| PBRM1 | LoF | Possible ICI benefit signal | C |
| FH | LoF (HLRCC-RCC) | Bevacizumab + erlotinib | B |
| TFE3 / TFEB | fusion (translocation RCC) | MET/VEGFR TKI trials | C |
