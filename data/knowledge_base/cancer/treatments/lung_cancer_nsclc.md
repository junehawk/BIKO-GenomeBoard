# Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer (NSCLC) — Treatment Overview

> Curated summary from CIViC / publicly-available guideline sources. Not official NCCN. For educational/decision-support context only.

## Targeted Therapy
- **EGFR exon 19 del / L858R**: Osimertinib first-line (Evidence: A)
- **EGFR exon 20 insertion**: Amivantamab or mobocertinib (Evidence: A)
- **ALK rearrangement**: Alectinib, brigatinib, or lorlatinib first-line (Evidence: A)
- **ROS1 rearrangement**: Crizotinib, entrectinib, or repotrectinib (Evidence: A)
- **BRAF V600E**: Dabrafenib + trametinib (Evidence: A)
- **KRAS G12C**: Sotorasib or adagrasib (second-line+) (Evidence: A)
- **MET exon 14 skipping / amplification**: Capmatinib or tepotinib (Evidence: A)
- **RET fusion**: Selpercatinib or pralsetinib (Evidence: A)
- **NTRK fusion**: Larotrectinib or entrectinib (Evidence: A)
- **HER2 (ERBB2) mutation**: Trastuzumab deruxtecan (Evidence: B)

## Immunotherapy
- **PD-L1 ≥50%, no driver**: Pembrolizumab monotherapy first-line (Evidence: A)
- **PD-L1 1–49%, no driver**: Chemo-immunotherapy (pembrolizumab + platinum doublet) (Evidence: A)
- **PD-L1 any level, no driver**: Nivolumab + ipilimumab ± chemo (Evidence: A)
- **Adjuvant (resected stage IB–IIIA, PD-L1 ≥1%)**: Atezolizumab (Evidence: A)

## Chemotherapy
- **Non-squamous**: Platinum + pemetrexed ± bevacizumab
- **Squamous**: Platinum + (gemcitabine or nab-paclitaxel)
- Used in combination with immunotherapy or for driver-negative progression
