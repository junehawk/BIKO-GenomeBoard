# Pancreatic Ductal Adenocarcinoma (PDAC) — Treatment Overview

> Curated summary from CIViC / publicly-available guideline sources. Not official NCCN. For educational/decision-support context only.

## Targeted Therapy
- **BRCA1/2 germline, platinum-responsive metastatic**: Olaparib maintenance (Evidence: A)
- **KRAS G12C mutant**: Sotorasib or adagrasib (Evidence: B)
- **KRAS G12D mutant**: Investigational (MRTX1133 and related) (Evidence: C)
- **NRG1 fusion**: Zenocutuzumab (Evidence: B)
- **NTRK fusion**: Larotrectinib or entrectinib (Evidence: A)
- **BRAF V600E**: Dabrafenib + trametinib (tumor-agnostic) (Evidence: B)
- **RET fusion**: Selpercatinib (tumor-agnostic) (Evidence: B)

## Immunotherapy
- **MSI-H / dMMR (rare, <1%)**: Pembrolizumab (Evidence: A)
- **TMB-high (≥10 mut/Mb)**: Pembrolizumab (tumor-agnostic) (Evidence: B)
- Monotherapy ICI is otherwise not effective in microsatellite-stable PDAC

## Chemotherapy
- **First-line (good PS)**: FOLFIRINOX or modified FOLFIRINOX (Evidence: A)
- **First-line (intermediate PS)**: Gemcitabine + nab-paclitaxel (Evidence: A)
- **Second-line**: Nanoliposomal irinotecan + 5-FU/LV; platinum if BRCA+
- **Adjuvant (resected)**: mFOLFIRINOX preferred
