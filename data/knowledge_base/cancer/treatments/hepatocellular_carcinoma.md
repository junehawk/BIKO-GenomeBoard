# Hepatocellular Carcinoma (HCC) — Treatment Overview

> Curated summary from CIViC / publicly-available guideline sources. Not official NCCN. For educational/decision-support context only.

## Targeted Therapy
- **First-line (Child–Pugh A)**: Sorafenib or lenvatinib (Evidence: A)
- **Second-line**: Regorafenib, cabozantinib, or ramucirumab (AFP ≥400 ng/mL) (Evidence: A)
- **FGF19-amplified / FGFR4-driven**: FGFR4 inhibitors (investigational) (Evidence: C)

## Immunotherapy
- **First-line preferred**: Atezolizumab + bevacizumab (IMbrave150) (Evidence: A)
- **First-line alternative**: Durvalumab + tremelimumab (HIMALAYA) (Evidence: A)
- **Second-line**: Nivolumab ± ipilimumab, pembrolizumab (Evidence: B)

## Chemotherapy
- Systemic cytotoxic chemotherapy has limited efficacy and is not standard
- Locoregional (TACE/TARE) remains standard for intermediate-stage disease

## Key Biomarkers
| Gene | Variant | Drug | Evidence |
|------|---------|------|----------|
| VEGF pathway | intact | Bevacizumab (+ atezolizumab) | A |
| AFP | ≥400 ng/mL | Ramucirumab | A |
| CTNNB1 | activating | Possible ICI resistance signal | C |
| TMB | ≥10 mut/Mb | Pembrolizumab (tumor-agnostic) | B |
| PD-L1 | any | Atezolizumab, durvalumab, nivolumab | A |
