# Gastric / Gastroesophageal Junction Adenocarcinoma — Treatment Overview

> Curated summary from CIViC / publicly-available guideline sources. Not official NCCN. For educational/decision-support context only.

## Targeted Therapy
- **HER2+ (IHC 3+ or IHC 2+/ISH+), first-line metastatic**: Trastuzumab + chemotherapy (+ pembrolizumab) (Evidence: A)
- **HER2+ second-line**: Trastuzumab deruxtecan (Evidence: A)
- **CLDN18.2+ (IHC ≥75% 2+/3+)**: Zolbetuximab + chemotherapy (Evidence: A)
- **FGFR2b+ (IHC ≥75% 2+/3+)**: Bemarituzumab (investigational) (Evidence: B)
- **VEGFR2 (any)**: Ramucirumab + paclitaxel (second-line) (Evidence: A)
- **NTRK fusion**: Larotrectinib or entrectinib (Evidence: A)

## Immunotherapy
- **PD-L1 CPS ≥1 or ≥5, first-line**: Nivolumab + chemotherapy (Evidence: A)
- **PD-L1 CPS ≥1, first-line**: Pembrolizumab + chemotherapy (± trastuzumab if HER2+) (Evidence: A)
- **MSI-H / dMMR**: Pembrolizumab (prioritize over chemo) (Evidence: A)
- **TMB-high (≥10 mut/Mb)**: Pembrolizumab (tumor-agnostic) (Evidence: B)

## Chemotherapy
- **First-line backbone**: Fluoropyrimidine (5-FU or capecitabine) + platinum (oxaliplatin or cisplatin)
- **Second-line**: Paclitaxel + ramucirumab, or irinotecan, or trifluridine/tipiracil
